Why Become Donor?

If you’re a blood donor, you’re a hero to someone, somewhere, who received your gracious gift of life.

The holidays are over and all the gifts have been exchanged — or have they? There's one gift remaining that is possibly the most important one you can give: blood.

Blood is our life force—literally. It contains many life-saving components that can help treat different illnesses and injuries. For some people with serious conditions, blood donors can play an essential part in their ongoing care. Blood donation often carries with it the feeling that you've helped someone in need and made a difference in his or her life in a most essential way.
