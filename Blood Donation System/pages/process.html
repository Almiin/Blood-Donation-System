Donation Process

Before donating blood, please eat regular meals, drink plenty of fluid (non-alcoholic) and avoid vigorous exercise or exertion.

Registration

  • You need to complete a very simple registration form. Which contains all required contact information.

Screening

  • A drop of blood from your finger will take for a simple test to ensure that your blood iron levels are proper enough for the donation process.

Donation

  • After ensuring and passed the screening test successfully you will be directed to a donor bed for donation.

Refreshment

  • You can also stay in sitting room until you feel strong enough to leave. You will receive an awesome drink from us.
